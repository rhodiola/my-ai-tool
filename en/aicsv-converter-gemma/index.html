Guidelines and limits
• Source CSV is expected to contain up to 100 rows.
• CSV files with too many columns or extremely large size may fail to process.
• Since the browser reads the CSV and checks its contents, very large CSV files may cause slower performance.
* The output CSV is generated in UTF-8 (with BOM) format and can be opened in standard editors or Excel.
* Because the conversion is performed automatically by AI, the result may not always be perfect. Please review the output data before use.
